Swap & Shop

Swap Saturdays

Each Saturday we meet in a different location around Ireland to Swap & Sell records. This is a family event and all are welcome.

Our friendly policy

Records can be brought to be swapped and sold by anyone who wishes. This is a fun day out aimed at enjoying music and company.

Fun for all the family

Bring the kids, bring the dogs! All our events take place in local city parks which are both kid and dog friendly. We would just remind you to please clean up

Soul Funk Disco Electronic

From old to new, we have appreciation for all types of music and we hope you do too. Bring along an old classic or swap it for your new favourite record.

We also do food!!

Food will be available at every swap meet, to encourage guests to relax and listen to some records for a few hours
